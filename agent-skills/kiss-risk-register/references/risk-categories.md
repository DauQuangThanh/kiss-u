# Risk categories

| Category | What it covers | Typical examples |
|---|---|---|
| **Technical** | Technology doesn't work as expected | Integration issues, vendor delays, performance fails under load |
| **Schedule** | Activities take longer than planned | Dependency slips, unavailable resources, estimate miss |
| **Resource** | People and skills | Key person leaves, skills gap, team burnout |
| **Budget** | Costs exceed forecast | Vendor price increases, unplanned expenses, scope-driven overruns |
| **Scope** | Uncontrolled scope expansion | Feature creep, changing requirements, stakeholder misalignment |
| **External** | Outside factors | Regulatory changes, market shifts, third-party outages |
| **Other** | Anything not fitting above | Political, organisational, environmental |

Log every risk with exactly one category. If a risk spans two
(e.g. a new regulation that drives both scope and schedule impact),
pick the dominant one and mention the secondary effect in the
description.
